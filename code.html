GreenHouse

Swift, XCode, GitHub, Figma

A mobile iOS application that aims to provide a more accessible way to teach people ways to be sustainable. This app can be used by people of all ages who are interested in taking small steps to change their way of living. Primarily worked on wireframing the interface and programming the application. Created as a final project for Kode With Klossy.

Carbon Footprint Calculator
